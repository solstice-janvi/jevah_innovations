How it Works.

01
Polymer Extraction

We extract high-purity Alginate from brown seaweed harvested from the Indian coastline, creating a 100% organic liquid container.

02
Spherification Lab

Using cold-spherification technology, we drop fortified water into a calcium bath to form a robust, dual-layer membrane.

03
Refinement & Peel

A secondary hygienic layer is added, designed to be peeled off like an orange skin before consumption.
