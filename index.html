Questions Answered

Common Inquiries.

Does it taste like seaweed?

No. The proprietary extraction process removes the naturally occurring salts and flavors from the alginate. The resulting membrane is entirely tasteless and simply takes on the profile of the water inside.

What is the shelf life of a GLOB?

Currently, our standard formulation maintains structural integrity and freshness for 3 to 4 weeks when stored in ambient conditions. We are actively developing next-gen formulations to extend this to 6 weeks.

How is it transported hygienically?

GLOBs are produced with a dual-layer membrane. The outer "peel" acts much like the skin of an orange—it protects the inner content during transit and handling. The user simply peels off the outer layer before consumption. We also transport them in bulk biodegradable crates.

Is it safe to swallow the membrane?

Absolutely. The inner membrane is 100% organic, vegan, and edible. It is made from brown seaweed extract (alginate) and calcium chloride, both of which are common, safe, FSSAI/FDA-approved food ingredients.
